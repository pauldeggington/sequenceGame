About Very Wild Jacks

Very Wild Jacks is a free online multiplayer board game inspired by the classic Sequence card game. Play with friends in real-time using peer-to-peer connections ‚Äî no accounts, no downloads, no servers storing your data.

How It Works

Simply create a room, share the invite link with your friends, and start playing! The game uses WebRTC technology through PeerJS to connect players directly to each other, ensuring fast and responsive gameplay.
