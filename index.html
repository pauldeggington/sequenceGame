How to Play

  • Goal: Form 2 sequences (1 if 3-teams) to win. Note: A sequence is 5 connected chips (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  • Play a card: Select a card from your hand and place a chip on the corresponding free space on the board.
  • Two-Eyed Jacks: These are WILD. They allow you to place a chip on ANY empty space.
  • One-Eyed Jacks: These are REMOVE cards. Pick any space occupied by an opponent's chip to take it off the board. (Note: You cannot remove chips that are already part of a completed sequence).
  • Free Spaces: The four corners (‚òÖ) are "free" spaces. They act as wild chips, meaning you only need 4 chips to build a sequence out of a corner.
